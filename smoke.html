WHY SHOULD YOU QUIT SMOKING?

  • Smoking affects all organs of the body including heart and blood vessels.
  • It also exposes the near and dear ones to the effects of passive smoking.

Is it useful to quit smoking after having a heart attack?

  • Yes. It is!!!

In the year after you quit smoking, your excess risk of coronary heart disease drops by 50%.

After 10 years, your risk is as low as that of someone who has never smoked.

HOW DO I QUIT SMOKING?

  • Set a quit date
  • Get rid of all cigarettes/ beedis/ tobacco from the house or vehicle or from work place.
  • The initial five minutes will be tough when the craving begins. Try to engage in healthy physical activities or use substitutes- sugarless gums/ mints.
  • If it s not working, medications prescribed (only!!!) by doctors can be sought.
