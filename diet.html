DIET PLAN

A well-maintained diet after angioplasty plays a crucial role in the process of recovery after an angioplasty procedure.

Foods to be Avoided:
  • Foods with a lot of salt (Chips, Pickles, salted snacks).
  • Fried Foods (Samosa,Poori,Chips,Bajji,bonda).
  • Sweets (Chocolates, ice creams, butter) and bakery products (Cakes, pastries and cream biscuits).
  • Carbonated drinks like coke, Pepsi and Fanta etc.
  • Red meats and Fatty meats (Mutton, Pork and Beef).
  • Saturated oils like Coconut oil and Palm oil.
  • Solid fats like Dalda and Margarines.

Foods to be Included:

  • Fruits and vegetables.
  • High fiber foods like whole grain cereals and whole sprouted pulses.
  • Fat free and low fat milk products.
  • Use non fried fish each week.
  • Unsalted nuts, seeds and legumes.
  • Vegetable oils like Corn, Olive or Safflower oils.
